# Evidence Grading Card

## Purpose

This card defines how the workflow separates exploratory genetic support from
confirmatory causal evidence.

## Interpretation Rules

- Lead-variant Wald ratio MR is interpreted as directional evidence.
- `PP.H4 > 0.8` is treated as strong colocalization support.
- `PP.H4 > 0.5` is treated as moderate colocalization support.
- Discordant MR and colocalization results are reported without forcing a
  causal conclusion.
- Potential UK Biobank overlap is treated as a limitation.
- Independent non-UKB replication is required for confirmatory claims.

## CRELD2 Application

CRELD2 showed directionally consistent MR evidence and moderate frailty
colocalization support, while telomere length and parental longevity did not
show comparable support. The final interpretation was therefore exploratory and
frailty-focused rather than a broad claim across all aging traits.
